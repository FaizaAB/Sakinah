# Sakinah

**Faith-aligned mental health support for Muslims**

Sakinah is a digital mental health platform designed to help Muslims access care that respects both **clinical needs** and **Islamic values**. The platform connects users with Muslim providers, scholar-informed guidance, community support, educational resources, and AI-assisted matching through **Khalil**.

---

## ✨ Vision

Many Muslims struggle to find mental health care that is both **clinically appropriate** and **religiously comfortable**. Sakinah was created to bridge that gap.

Our goal is to make support more accessible by combining:
- a trusted network of Muslim mental health providers
- scholar-informed Islamic guidance
- intelligent matching based on user needs
- community and educational support
- financial assistance pathways

---

## 🌿 What Sakinah Offers

### Provider Matching
Users can be matched with Muslim providers across clinics and private practice based on:
- location
- insurance
- language
- provider gender preference
- specialty
- telehealth or in-person availability

### Islamic Guidance
When needed, users can access scholar-informed guidance that helps them navigate therapy in a way that aligns with their faith.

### AI Support with Khalil
**Khalil** is the AI-powered intake assistant built into the platform. Khalil helps users:
- start building their profile
- share their needs in a conversational way
- begin the matching process
- improve future matches through feedback after visits

### Community Support
Sakinah includes a moderated community space where users can:
- share stories
- ask questions
- learn from others
- find encouragement and belonging

### Education Hub
The platform also includes educational content rooted in Islamic tradition, including:
- reflections on emotional well-being
- lessons on grief, patience, hardship, and resilience
- stories from the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ and the Sahabah

### Financial Assistance
Sakinah is designed to support users who may not be able to afford therapy through:
- subsidized care
- hardship-based support
- donor-funded assistance
- nonprofit and community partnerships

---

## 🧭 Core Pathways

Sakinah is built around three main support pathways:

1. **Find a Muslim Provider**  
   Get matched with a provider who understands Islamic values and mental health needs.

2. **Receive Scholar-Informed Guidance**  
   Access a faith-sensitive framework when navigating treatment decisions.

3. **Join Community & Education**  
   Learn, connect, and heal through shared experiences and trusted content.

---

## 🤖 Why Khalil Matters

Khalil is not a replacement for care — it is the first step toward it.

Khalil helps make intake feel:
- easier
- more personal
- less overwhelming
- more responsive to each user’s needs

By learning from user feedback after appointments, Khalil helps the platform improve future matches over time.
